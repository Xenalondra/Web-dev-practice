Upcoming Kdrama 2024
Queen of Divorce
follows the story of Sara Kim (Lee Ji Ah), Korea’s greatest divorce problem solver, and her business partner Dong Ki Joon (Kang Ki Young), an eccentric lawyer, as they fearlessly bring justice to “bad spouses” with their solutions.
Wonderful World
Upcoming MBC drama that follows the story of Eun Soo-Hyun (Kim Nam-Joo), who uncovers the mystery surrounding her son’s death.
Connection
A crime investigation thriller K-drama that follows the story of two friends investigating their friend’s death. This friend from high school leaves around KRW 5 billion in insurance.
Nine Puzzle
The series follows the story of a woman, Yi-Na (Kim Da-Mi), who was the only one to witness her uncle’s death when she was a child. She meets Detective Han-Saem (Son Suk-Ku) while working as a criminal profiler 10 years later. He suspects her of killing her uncle.
Hong Rang
An upcoming Korean period thriller series set to release on Netflix. It follows the story of a wealthy merchant family in the Joseon Era. The series will document the story of a brother, Hong Rang (Lee Jae-Wook), who suddenly disappears and returns home after 10 years without any memory of his childhood.
